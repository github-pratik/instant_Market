About Instant Market

Our Mission

Instant Market is a dedicated platform for GMU students to buy and sell items within the university community. Our mission is to create a safe, convenient, and sustainable marketplace that helps students save money and reduce waste by facilitating the exchange of textbooks, electronics, furniture, and other essentials.
